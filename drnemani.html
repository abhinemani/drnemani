Meet Dr. Nemani

Dr. Sajjan K. Nemani is a Neurologist with specialization in Neurophysiology and Sleep Medicine. He has brought together team of dedicated, skilled healthcare professionals at Advanced Sleep and Neurodiagnostic Services to provide you with quality care. At our sleep labs and offices, we offer a wide range of services. We offer general neurology consultation and neurophysiological testing, including EEGs, EMGs, Nerve conduction studies, Evoked potentials, and Video-Electronystagmography. At our sleep clinics we offer sleep disorder services, including in-lab sleep testing, CPAP Titration and management, multiple sleep latency tests, and home sleep testing (HST). With a number of locations across the Southern Illinois area, we are here to help.

Board Certified

Dr. Nemani is certified by the American Board of Neurology with an added qualification in Clinical Neurophysiology and Sleep Medicine (ABPN), and he is a Fellow Member of the American Academy of Neurology. He is also a Diplomat of the American Board of Sleep Medicine (ABSM) and the American Board of Neuromuscular and Electrodiagnostic Medicine (AANEM).

General Neurology Consultation

We provide in-office general neurology consultation for Seizures, Headaches, Dizziness, Gait disorders, Dementias, Parkinson’s Disease, Stroke, Neuromuscular Disorders, etc.

EEG, EMG & VNG

An EEG records the electrical activity of the brain; a 24 hour ambulatory EEG is also available. An EMG and Nerve Conduction Study are a tests that is used to record the electrical activity of nerves and muscles. VNG is utilized for diagnosis of patients with dizziness or inner-ear disturbance.

Sleep Testing & Consultation

We offer comprehensive sleep consultation: in-lab, overnight sleep testing (Polysomnography), CPAP Titration and management, multiple sleep latency tests, and home sleep testing (HST).
